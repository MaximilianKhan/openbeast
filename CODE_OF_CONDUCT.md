# Code of Conduct

OpenBeast follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The short version: be excellent to each other. Technical disagreement is
welcome — argued with evidence, benchmarks, and reproductions. Personal
attacks, harassment, and bad-faith conduct are not, anywhere in the
project's spaces.

Enforcement contact: the maintainer, via the channels in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports are handled confidentially.
